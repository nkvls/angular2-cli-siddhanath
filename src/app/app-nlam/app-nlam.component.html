Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is this a religion or cult?
A: It is neither. It is a way of life celebrating soulfulness - the joy felt in your body, mind and soul. Everyone can participate in this celebration, irrespective of creed or beliefs.

Q: Are there any charges or hidden fees to get the full techniques?
A: You are receiving the complete NLAM system at this time – it is completely free. From time to time, we may add techniques or augment the existing ones – theses will also be provided free.

Q: Why are you providing these precious techniques free?
A: It is the blessing of the Himalayan Master Yogiraj Siddhanath to help humanity find happiness and develop self peace for the sake of earth peace.

Q: Are there any precautions about doing these techniques?
A: There are no restrictions for the Siddhanath Earth Peace and Golden Lotus techniques. One should refrain form performing the Siddhanath Surya Yoga with a full stomach – it should be done before meals or at least 2 hours after food.

Q: Can pregnant women do these techniques?
A: Yes, but refrain from any jerky movements during Siddhanth Surya Yoga.

Q: I've downloaded the videos. Now, which one should I do first?
A: Siddhanath Surya, followed by Golden lotus and then Earth peace.

Q: How often should I do these techniques to get results?
A: Once a day in the morning and if time permits, then add an evening session as well. Results come quickly but remember that this is a life-long practice of healing and wholing

Q: How long should I practice Surya before adding the Golden lotus and / or Earth Peace?
A: Practice half an hour for five days and then add the Golden lotus followed by five more days of practice before adding the Earth Peace.

Q: Can we learn the Surya mantra from a teacher?
